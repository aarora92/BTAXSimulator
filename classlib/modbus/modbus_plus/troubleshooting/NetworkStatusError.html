Troubleshooting

"Network Status" Error Indictation on Modbus Master

The Modbus Master has either lost communication to the network, or is trying to read from an area of the network that is not accessable. The first step is to check the Modbus routing path in the ModbusSA85 class, and be sure it is valid.

If communication is lost, or could not be established at startup it could be a problem with the SA85 card, a problem with the network or a problem with the MBX Driver. See "MBX Driver Help" - "Troubleshooting the MBX Driver".

To be sure automationX is trying to read from a valid node, or to test that the MBX Driver is functioning correctly, see "MBX Driver Help" - "Verification of the Driver Operation".
